High volume, high standards, low costs

Printing copious amounts of excellent borderless images up to A4 size2 at a studio, at home, or at the workplace is made easy with the Canon PIXMA G550. With six refillable dye-based ink tanks that last longer between refills, this high-volume printer has a lower total cost of ownership. The non-squeeze bottles with mechanical ID ensure a mess-free and error-free ink replacement method when the time comes. The maintenance cartridge is also easy to replace at home or in the studio, which reduces downtime on busy days. You'll save time since you can print from your phone or tablet using the Canon PRINT App, Mopria, or AirPrint, or connect over Wi-Fi to your device.
